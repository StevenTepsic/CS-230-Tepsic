# CS-230-Tepsic
CS-230 Class project repository

The Gaming Room needs a web-based version of their gaming app, “Draw It or Lose It”. The game is currently available on Android, and The Gaming Room wants to serve multiple platforms.

One of the things I doccumented best was the programs needs and requirements.

One of the helpful parts about working through the design doccument was thinking about the advantages and disadvantages that different platforms provided for the project.

One thing I could have done better was updating the dates and versions of the software design doccument as I incremented it.

I interpreted the user's needs by making sure to understand their requirements and defining them into clear testable goals like being "web based" or "multiplatform"

It is important to consider the user's needs becasue they are the ones who are going to be using the program, and if it can not fulfill their needs, it has failed in its objective.

I approached software design by first defining the projects needs, then evaluating different platforms for their strenghts and weaknesses to the projects needs. Finally, I combined the data to provide a recccomendation based of needs and functionality.
